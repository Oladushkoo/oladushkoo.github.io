Roses


White Rose color The white rose is a symbol of harmony, purity, love, fidelity and innocence. Presenting these flowers, the donor seems to be saying to the addressee - "You are heavenly and pure, I admire your pristine and unearthly beauty! You are as perfect as these flowers!". A white rose is a symbol of strong, pure and eternal love, which is why they are given for a wedding. Rose Red The meaning of the color of roses red means true, passionate love and desire. Many people know that the smell of pink and red roses is amazing. He expresses passion and deep love. Also, these flowers are presented as a sign of admiration and respect. Red and white roses combined in one bouquet mean harmonious love, unanimity, strong and long friendship, union.

Piones


The meaning of this symbol is not only love, but also wealth, prosperity. It is the national flower of China, where it is known as the "flower of wealth and honor". It means affection, feminine beauty and the loving feelings of youth. Peonies are often mentioned in romantic Chinese poetry and literature and are used as the main motifs of decorative art, their image can be found on porcelain and tapestries. Cream, pale pink and snow-white tones symbolize purity, innocence and sincerity. Based on this, light peonies are appropriate at the following events: At the wedding. In the East, the shape of the ball means harmony and peace. Look at the inflorescence of the peony: even when it blooms, it resembles a terry ball. Wish a cloudless marriage to the newlyweds with a beautiful bouquet. On a date at the beginning of a relationship. Usually men give roses. Be creative and show that you are not like the others. The girl definitely does not expect to see a bouquet of several delicate buds in your hands when they meet. On the birthday of an older woman. Choose not white, but pink or peach flowers. With the help of a present, you will wish the birthday girl longevity, health and well-being.

Gerbera


Gerbera is a universal flower. It is suitable for a gift to people with whom the giver is in different relationships. It can be a beloved woman or a relative, a friend or a work colleague. In the language of flowers, such a plant means a smile and a good mood. It can also act as a symbol of flirting. In parallel, Gerber is endowed with secrecy and secrecy. In order to clearly convey feelings and wishes, it is necessary to choose the right gerberas by color: Red - to express passionate love; Pink – young women with wishes of joy; Yellow - for a gift that can attract good luck; Orange - as a symbol of well-being in all spheres of life; White - the choice for a wedding bouquet or for innocent girls; Dark saturated tones – for a gift to an adult, a woman who has taken place in her life.

Tulip


Spring tulip flower means: happiness; love; well-being; reconciliation; devotion. There was a period in history when they were indicators of luxury and wealth. During the period of tulip mania, for the sale of several bulbs of a special variety or type, they could get jewelry, a ship or a whole house! Bouquets of tulips are perfect as a gift for absolutely any celebration: Birthday; anniversary; the wedding; engagement; first date. Competent florists will definitely help you create the right composition in all respects. They decorate bouquets with various accessories, packaging, ribbons and lace in accordance with the theme of the celebration.

Lily


White (the most popular) denote purity and innocence. Therefore, if there is trust in the language of flowers, you should not give a bouquet of white lilies to older women. Pink lilies are predictable – they speak of the tenderness and romantic mood of the giver. It will be a good gift for either a young girl or an adult woman who wants to make a compliment as a warm, affectionate, kind and endearing person. Orange lilies look bright, look rich and optimistic - in fact, they are given as a wish for health and longevity. It is more than appropriate to give such a bouquet to a person who was ill, but is already on the way to recovery. There is no fading, fragility and vulnerability in it (and it is better not to show it to the sick even in the form of flowers, the subconscious mind works subtly), but there is motivation, mood, charging brightness. The blue plant speaks of destiny, of the turns of fate prescribed from above. That is, a bouquet of such lilies can mean acceptance: willingness to accept what is happening in life with gratitude, and work with what is. If you give examples, then forced relocation, dismissal, difficulties that cannot be fixed in one day - this is the reason to present a bouquet of blue or blue lilies to a loved one as a sign that we will cope anyway.
